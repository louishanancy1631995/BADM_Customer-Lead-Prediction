Customer-Lead-Prediction
This project uses machine learning techniques to predict customer behavior for an edtech company, analyzing factors such as engagement, demographics, and course completion rates. 
The project employs predictive models to identify potential churn, cross-selling, and upselling opportunities, helping the company to optimize its customer engagement strategies. 
The results are presented in a clear and concise manner, allowing the company to make data-driven decisions and improve its overall customer experience.
